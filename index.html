The route to success for Graduates in Wales

Make the best start to your career with GO Wales – paid work placements, work experience, jobs and training.

Services

Jobs and Placements

Work Placements are paid work lasting 6-10 weeks combined with a work-based learning award.

Jobs Growth Wales are paid jobs for at least 6 months for those 24 and under.

Graduate Jobs is a comprehensive job search facility dedicated to the Welsh graduate recruitment market.

Work Experience

Work Tasters are unpaid, quality work experience relating to your career goals.

Voluntary Internships are unpaid and can provide you with an opportunity to gain new skills and added experience during short term positions.

Funding and Training

Graduate Academy is a free programme focussing on work related skills and employability.

Freelancer Academy is a training course designed to help you to become a successful freelancer.

Funding for Training provides graduates working in SMEs with 50% off training costs.
